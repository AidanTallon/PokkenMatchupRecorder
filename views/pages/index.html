Pokken Matchup Recorder is a tool that allows you to save and share your opinions on matchups in Pokkén Tournament

Created by Aidan Tallon

×

Click a Pokémon to get started!

Record matchups by selecting two Pokémon and a matchup value and hitting Submit.

You can delete matchups by pressing the Delete Matchup button with two Pokémon selected.

Or you can delete many matchups at once by pressing Clear in the top right corner.

Matchup charts can be saved to and loaded from your computer.

Click Share and then choose a Pokémon to generate a matchup chart image.

Save this image to your computer so you can share it with others.
